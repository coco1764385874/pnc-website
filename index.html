Welcome to PNC
Cambodia
2026
Launched in 2005 in Phnom Penh, Passerelles Numériques Cambodia (PNC) offers a 2-year IT training program in IT, based on
a holistic approach including technical skills and professional development (or soft skills). While at PNC, the basic needs of our
students (housing, food, medical care) are covered.
PNC Value
For Student PNC follow

Responsibility

As a PNC student, this means owning your actions, completing assignments on time, and contributing to a positive classroom environment. It’s about being accountable for your learning and growth.

Trust

This is the confidence you build with teachers and peers at PNC by being honest and reliable. Trust allows you to collaborate on projects and seek help when needed, creating a supportive community.

Sodality

This refers to a sense of fellowship or unity among PNC students. It’s about forming strong bonds with classmates, participating in group activities, and working together toward common goals.

Respect

At PNC, this means valuing your teachers, classmates, and yourself. It involves listening attentively, appreciating diverse opinions, and treating the learning space with care.
